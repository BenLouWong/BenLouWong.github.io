About Us

Developed as a passion project, The Coffee Project started off as an idea of providing nothing except quality coffee recommendations for everyone.



Why Us?

Sydney has one of the most vibrant coffee scenes in the world. From franchises to hole-in-the-wall venues, most places you can drop into serve world class, exceptional coffee. Where should you start?


Designed, developed and reviewed by fellow caffeine addicts The Coffee Project aims to provide provide indepth and detailed reviews for all batch bros, latte lovers and cappuccino characters alike!
